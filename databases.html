Databases and data modeling

I design structured, scalable database systems focused on performance, data integrity and maintainability.

Data modeling is the process of organizing information before development begins. It defines how different pieces of data relate to each other and how they should be structured for efficiency and clarity.
Good data modeling prevents future problems such as, slow application performance, complex or fragile systems, expensive redesigns later.

Data modeling for applications
